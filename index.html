Jonathan Carlson's Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! Here you will see all work, current and past, that I feel best represents my abilities and skills from the years of knowledge and skill that I have put into the industry of Game Design and Art.

My Name is Jonathan Carlson, I was born and raised in Fitchburg Massachusetts. Ever since I was a kid I always loved computers and games, I had a true passion for art and drawing and found that Game Design was an oppurtunity for me to ustilized all my skills in one career. I started my four years of High School at Montachusett Regional Vocational Technical Highschool in Westminster Massachusetts, there I studied Information technology and achieved various certifications, in PC maintence and Repair, Networking, Mobile Application Development, and Web Design. I also spent my four years competing in Skills USA; a competition which places tech schools around the world against each other in various competions of skill. In my case I competed in 3D Animation and Visualization and took silver twice in the state wide competion. I also spent my four years delving deep into Servers, learning the ins and outs of server maintence and setup, including file servers, and web servers alike. I became well aquainted with all operating systems including Windows, MAC OS, Arch, Debian, Cent OS, and Ubuntu. After Graduating I started school at Fitchburg State University in a four year program to achieve a Bachelors in Computer Science/Game Design, with a minor in Art. I have become proficent in applications like Photoshop, Unity, Zbrush, Maya, and After Effects. I strive to do and be the best I can be and take time to learn the ins and outs of every program.
