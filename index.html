Jonathan Carlson's Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! Here you will see all work, current and past, that I feel best represents my abilities and skills from the years of knowledge and skill that I have put into the industry of Game Design and Art.

My Name is Jonathan Carlson and I was born and raised in Fitchburg Massachusetts. Since childhood I always loved computers, video agems, and have always had a true passion for art. I find that Game Design is an oppurtunity that allows me to utilize all my skills in one promising career.

I started my High School career at Montachusett Regional Vocational Technical Highschool in Westminster, Massachusetts. I studied Information technology and achieved various certifications, in PC maintence and Repair, Networking, Mobile Application Development, and Web Design. I competed in Skills USA; a competition in which technical schools around the world are placed against each other in various competions of skill. In my case, I competed in 3D Animation and Visualization where I placed silver twice in the state wide competions. During my four years of high school, I spent my time delving deep into Servers, learning the ins-and-outs of server maintenance and setup, including file servers, and web servers alike. I became well acquainted with all operating systems, including Windows, MAC OS, Arch, Debian, Cent OS, and Ubuntu.

After Graduating I furthered my education by enrolling in Fitchburg State University. I took on a four year program to acquire a Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Game Design, with a minor in Art. In my years of college, I have become proficent in applications such as Photoshop, Unity, Zbrush, Maya, and After Effects.

I have spent alot of my time outside of schooling woring on my artistic abilities. I play video games while I stream on twitch, and have done numerous pieces of artwork, including animated and static for both my channel and others. I have become very successful on twitch, and have even joined numerous esport organizations while streaming. Overall I strive to do and be the best I can be and take time to learn the ins-and-outs of every program. Below You can find some of my work, broken into corresponding sections.
